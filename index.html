ABOUT


Our club "CODE ARMY" is the official coding and programming club of our college IIT Dholakpur. CODE ARMY was established in the year 2017, with 20 members from different branches in the college such as Computer Science, Electronics and Communication, Information Technology etc.

Today we have more than 100 members from different years across all the branches. We work with many fields in Computer Sciences like Web-Development, Competitive Programming, Ethical Hacking, Android Development. Memebers of the club have experience in many programming lamguages and technologies like C++, Python, React.js, Flutter, JavaScript etc.

Joining the club will be beneficial for you as you will be having interaction with seniors and alumni working at respectable places with loads of experience. Working with the club also develops many skills in a student which are helpful in their career ahead.




REGISTRATION PROCESS





The registrations or auditions for the club happens once a year. The audition process consist of three steps.

  1. Aptitude Round
  2. Project Showcasing Round
  3. Personal Interview Round

Those who clear all the rounds are selected to the club.
